# pordl — Acceptable Use & Liability (starter clause)

> Paste into your ToS at pordl.dev. **Not legal advice.** Before meaningful
> revenue, get one hour with a lawyer on the indemnification wording.

**1. Nature of the service.** pordl retrieves a web resource **at your
direction** and returns it as text. pordl reads only from a curated set of
sources whose licenses permit reuse (public-domain, Creative Commons, and
permissively-licensed material). pordl does not host or maintain a copy of
destination content. Each request is initiated by you and executed on your behalf.

**2. Caller responsibility.** You are responsible for ensuring your use of any
retrieved content complies with the applicable source license (including
attribution and share-alike terms, which pordl returns with each response) and
all applicable laws.

**3. Prohibited uses.** You may not use pordl to: circumvent any access control
or bot-detection; conduct bulk or systematic extraction of a single source;
access non-public or private network resources; or rebuild, mirror, or compete
with a source's offering.

**4. Source restrictions.** pordl reads only from sources it has chosen to
permit, and may add or remove sources at any time, including at a source
operator's request.

**5. Attribution.** Each response includes the source and its license. Where a
license requires attribution or share-alike (e.g. CC BY / CC BY-SA), you must
comply when reusing the content.

**6. Indemnification.** You will indemnify and hold pordl harmless from any
claim arising out of your use of the service.

**7. No warranty.** The service is provided "as is." pordl does not warrant the
accuracy, completeness, or lawfulness of any retrieved content.
